# Phishing Email Investigation

## Scenario
A user reports a suspicious email asking them to reset their password.

## Investigation Steps
1. Checked sender email
2. Analyzed link in the email
3. Looked up domain reputation
4. Verified if the domain was malicious

## Tools Used
VirusTotal
Email header analyzer

## Findings
The domain was recently registered and flagged as malicious.

## Conclusion
This email was a phishing attempt designed to steal credentials.
