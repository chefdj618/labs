# Phishing Email Investigation

## Scenario
A user reports a suspicious email asking them to reset their password.

## Investigation Steps
1. Checked sender email domain
2. Analyzed email headers
3. Investigated link destination
4. Checked domain reputation

## Tools Used
- VirusTotal
- MXToolbox
- Email Header Analyzer

## Findings
The sender domain was newly registered and flagged by multiple threat intelligence sources.

## Conclusion
The email was confirmed to be a phishing attempt designed to harvest credentials.
